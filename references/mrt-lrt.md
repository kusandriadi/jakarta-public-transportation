# MRT Jakarta, LRT Jakarta & LRT Jabodebek

Three separate rail systems. Don't confuse **LRT Jakarta** (small city line in
Kelapa Gading–Rawamangun) with **LRT Jabodebek** (big regional line to
Cibubur/Bekasi). They are different operators and not directly connected.

---

## MRT Jakarta

### North–South Line (Lin Utara–Selatan) — OPERATING
**Lebak Bulus Grab ↔ Bundaran HI Bank DKI**, 13 stations (Phase 1, since 2019).
Per-station Transjakarta/rail integrations (from official MRT-J system map, ~2026):

1. Lebak Bulus Grab (Bank Syariah Indonesia) — *terminus, depot.* ↔ TJ **8, 7A, 6H, S21, S22, OK-3, OK-32**
2. Fatmawati (Indomaret) — ↔ TJ **7A, 1E, 6H, S12, S22, S31, OK-31**
3. Cipete Raya (Tuku) — ↔ TJ **1E**
4. Haji Nawi — ↔ TJ **1E**
5. Blok A — ↔ TJ **1E**
6. Blok M (BCA) — *Blok M terminus.* ↔ TJ **1, 1C, 1E, 1Q, 6M, 6N, 7B, 8D, 8E, 9H, 13A, S21, S31**
7. ASEAN — ↔ TJ **1, 6M, S21, 13, 13A, 13B, 13C, 13D, 13E**
8. Senayan (Mastercard) — ↔ TJ **1, 1F, 1P, 4C, 6M, 9C, S12, S21, T11, GR1, BW4**
9. Istora (Mandiri) — ↔ TJ **1, 1F, 1P, 4C, 6M, S21**
10. Bendungan Hilir (Benhil) — ↔ TJ **1, 18, 4C, 6D, 6E, 9D, GR1, S21**
11. Setiabudi (Astra) — ↔ TJ **GR1**
12. Dukuh Atas (BNI) — *super-hub: ↔ **KRL Commuter Line**, **KA Bandara Soekarno-Hatta (BNI City)**, LRT Jabodebek; ↔ TJ **1, 4, 6, 7B, 1P, 4A, 4C, 6A, 6B, 9B, 9D, 13A, 13C, B11, S21, GR1***
13. Bundaran HI (Bank DKI) — *terminus.* ↔ TJ **1, 1P, 6A, 6B, 5A, 9B, 9D, GR1, BW**

**Fares:** distance-based, **Rp 3.000 (antar-stasiun bersebelahan) → Rp 14.000
(ujung-ke-ujung, Lebak Bulus ↔ Dukuh Atas/Bundaran HI)**. Perkiraan — arahkan ke
**app MRT-J** untuk tarif pasti. Dalam satu perjalanan Jak Lingko ≤3 jam, cap
integrasi **TJ+MRT+LRT Rp 10.000** bisa bikin perjalanan gabungan lebih murah
dari tarif MRT sendiri (lihat `fares.md`).

**Under construction / planned:**
- **Phase 2A** Bundaran HI → Kota (7 stations, ~2028): Sarinah, Monas, Harmoni,
  Sawah Besar, Mangga Besar, Glodok, Kota.
- **Phase 2B** Kota → Ancol (study).

### East–West Line (Lin Timur–Barat) — UNDER CONSTRUCTION
Phase 3A (Stage 1): **Medan Satria (Bekasi) → Tomang**, 21 stations / ~24.5 km,
groundbreaking Sept 2024. Will eventually run Balaraja (west) ↔ Cikarang (east).
Not yet operational.

## LRT Jakarta (city line) — OPERATING
**Pegangsaan Dua ↔ Velodrome**, 6 stations (Phase 1A):

1. Pegangsaan Dua — *terminus, depot (Kelapa Gading)*
2. Boulevard Utara
3. Boulevard Selatan
4. Pulomas
5. Equestrian
6. Velodrome (Rawamangun) — *terminus; ↔ Transjakarta via skybridge*

**Phase 1B (building, ~2026):** Velodrome → Manggarai, adding Rawamangun,
Pramuka BPKP, Pasar Pramuka, Matraman, Manggarai.

## LRT Jabodebek (regional) — OPERATING
Two lines sharing the inner trunk **Dukuh Atas BNI ↔ Cawang**, then splitting.

### Shared trunk (Dukuh Atas → Cawang)
Dukuh Atas BNI → Setiabudi → Rasuna Said → Kuningan → Pancoran (Bank BJB) →
Cikoko → Ciliwung → **Cawang** (junction)

### Cibubur Line (Cawang → Harjamukti)
Cawang → TMII → Kampung Rambutan → Ciracas → **Harjamukti** *(terminus, Cibubur)*

### Bekasi Line (Cawang → Jati Mulya)
Cawang → Halim → Jati Bening Baru → Cikunir 1 → Cikunir 2 → Bekasi Barat →
**Jati Mulya** *(terminus, Bekasi)*

**Interchanges:** Dukuh Atas BNI (↔ MRT, KRL Sudirman, KA Bandara, Transjakarta);
Cawang (↔ Transjakarta, walk to KRL Cawang); Kampung Rambutan & Harjamukti (↔
Transjakarta).
